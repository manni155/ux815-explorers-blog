# Post 1: Ignition
{% tagline %}

<div class="content">I'm sitting here in the cockpit, the light of my homeworld reflecting off the console. The engines are primed, the navigational computer is humming, and the void of space is calling to me. I've been dreaming of this moment for years, since I was a little boy staring up at the night sky. Now, it's finally here.

I'm leaving behind everything I know, everyone I love. I'm leaving behind the only planet I've ever called home. But I'm also moving towards something new, something exciting, something that's been waiting for me. I'm going to explore the universe, discover new worlds, and see things that no one has ever seen before.

I'm not afraid. I'm excited. I'm ready. I'm going to make this journey my own, and I'm going to make it one to remember.

So, here's to the ignition, to the stars, and to the unknown. I'm going to see what's out there.</div>
